SAT & PSAT
SAT is a Scholastic Aptitude test that qualifies a student to study in the United States of America, Canada and Australia.
It is meant to confirm the suitability of international candidates for university admission and to facilitate their individual application for scholarships at various undergraduate levels in American and Canadian universities.
There are basically two test papers: SAT I, which is a Reasoning Test and has papers in English and Mathematics and SAT II, papers which depend on the student’s choice of universities and majors.
If required, a maximum of three subjects would be picked, which must be in line with the candidate’s choice of major/course. PSAT is taken by SSI and SS2 students as a way of grooming them for SAT Exams.
